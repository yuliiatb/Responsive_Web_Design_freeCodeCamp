Main events in Pablo Picasso's life

  • 1881 - Born in Málaga, Spain.
  • 1891 - His family moves to A Coruña, Spain, where his father became a professor at the School of Fine Arts.
  • 1895 - Picasso's seven-year-old sister Conchita dies of diphtheria, which traumatizes the artist.
  • 1894 - He begins his carreer as a painter.
  • 1900 - Picasso travels to Paris for the first time.
  • 1903 - Paints La Vie, a gloomy allegorical painting that is now displayed at Cleveland Museum of Art (Ohio, USA).
  • 1927 - Started an affair with Marie-Thérèse Walter and had a daughter, Maya, with her. However, they never got married.
  • 1881 - Born in Málaga, Spain.
  • 1937 - Picasso paints Guernica, one of his most famous works. This large canvas embodies for many the inhumanity, brutality and hopelessness of war. It is now displayed at Museo Reina Sofia in Madrid, Spain.
  • 1973 - The artist dies at the age of 91 in France.
